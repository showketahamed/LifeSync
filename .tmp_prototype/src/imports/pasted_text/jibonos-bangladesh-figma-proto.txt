Create a complete high-fidelity Figma UI/UX prototype for a Bangladesh-focused personal life management web application named:

“JibonOS Bangladesh”

Tagline:
“Manage Your Life, All in One Place”

Project Overview:
JibonOS Bangladesh is a modern personal life management platform designed for Bangladeshi students, young professionals, and families. The platform combines task management, personal finance, bill reminders, government document tracking, academic planning, goals, family responsibilities, health reminders, and AI-powered personal assistance in one secure dashboard.

The application will later be developed using Next.js, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, Shadcn UI, PostgreSQL, Prisma, and Auth.js. Therefore, create a clean, component-based, developer-friendly design that can be easily converted into reusable Next.js components.

Target Users:

1. University students in Bangladesh
2. Young professionals
3. Freelancers
4. Families managing household responsibilities
5. Users managing bills, documents, study plans, and financial goals

Design Style:

* Modern SaaS dashboard
* Clean and professional
* Minimal but visually engaging
* Friendly and easy to understand
* Suitable for both Bangla and English
* Mobile-first and fully responsive
* Use cards, charts, icons, tabs, progress bars, calendars, and clean forms
* Avoid excessive gradients and unnecessary visual clutter
* Use generous spacing and rounded cards
* Use subtle shadows and soft borders
* Maintain strong accessibility and readable typography
